Entire library is released with three commands:
mvn release:clean
mvn release:prepare
mvn release:perform


After that, follow steps to release:
https://docs.sonatype.org/display/Repository/Sonatype+OSS+Maven+Repository+Usage+Guide#SonatypeOSSMavenRepositoryUsageGuide-8.ReleaseIt

After that, update and test all examples to the latest released version
